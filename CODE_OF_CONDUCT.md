# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and considerate
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
